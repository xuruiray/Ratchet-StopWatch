Ratchet

A mechanical feedback app for the round StopWatch device. Drag the gear, flick it into inertia, or trigger rotation from the hardware buttons while sound and vibration stay gated to the same ratchet rhythm.

Ratchet
0 deg/s

Interaction model

Pointer rotation maps directly to gear angle while button input gives the gear a full-speed impulse. Releasing a drag continues with short mechanical inertia and then decays back to rest.

  • Drag on the gear to rotate it manually.
  • Press A or B from rest to spin left or right.
  • Press A+B during inertia to stop rotation.

Hardware parity

The browser prototype mirrors the firmware constants used by the app: nine teeth, inertia gating, audio click timing, and short vibration pulses are all designed to stay synchronized.

  • Feedback is rate-limited instead of queued.
  • Fast rotation may skip teeth to preserve timing.
  • Embed mode stays a clean 466 x 466 device surface.
